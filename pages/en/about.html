A Journey from animation to Web Development

I began my career as a sports monitor at Aventure Parc Waver. where I welcomed, equipped, and guided visitors through treetop courses, ensuring their safety while enhancing their experience. I was also in charge of organizing and hosting birthday parties for younger children who couldn't climb. Over time, I had the opportunity to take on more responsibilities, becoming a bungee jump monitor and eventually a teambuilding manager. During off-peak seasons, we handled gardening and maintenance tasks to keep the park in top shape. One of the most demanding but rewarding projects I participated in was the implementation of a continuous lifeline system, a major safety upgrade for the park.

Retail Experience: Leadership and Strategy

Before transitioning to web development, I spent over 10 years in Retail, starting as a cashier at in Action Perwez. Eager for challenges, I quickly became an assistant manager, learning the ins and outs of team leadership, stock management, and commercial strategy. However, as my values no longer aligned with the company’s approach to people management, I moved to Hema, where I found a more fulfilling role.

At Hema, I spent two years as an assistant manager for a team of 15 before being promoted to store manager. This position allowed me to develop and implement commercial strategies while maintaining a strong focus on customer service and team cohesion. My team and I navigated through challenging times, including the COVID-19 crisis and severe flooding, reinforcing my ability to adapt, lead, and think creatively under pressure.

Passion for Board Games & Event Organization

Board games have always held a special place in my heart. After a period where work and studies took priority, I decided to realign my life with my passion. In 2022, I took the leap and combined my skills in event organization, animation, and gaming to create Dés-mentiel. This project is a perfect reflection of my strengths and passions, allowing me to bring people together through playful experiences.
My journey took an exciting turn when I discovered Wanna Play and its passionate community. Enthralled by their vision, I quickly joined as a volunteer, contributing my energy and creativity to the organization of unforgettable gaming events, including the Wanna Play in Brussels 2022 festival. I love sharing my passion and introducing others to this amazing world—whether with family, friends, or fellow enthusiasts.
